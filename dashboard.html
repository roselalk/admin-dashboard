Reasons I Want A MacBook

Performance

The MacBook Pro (2021) performs 2.4 times faster than the Surface Laptop 3. Can you imagine getting 2.4 times more work done in a day?

Unix Based

MacBooks are built on a Unix system. Unix has proved itself as the most stable basis for an OS, meaning the machines do not crash as much. It's also known for posessing much greater processing power than Windows.

Frontend Developer Tooling

Design Software

Apple offers Sketch, a powerful design tool used within the KvK as well as being an industry standard. This would offer me more space to explore the field of UI/UX design.

Battery life

The MacBook Pro (2021) features a 27% bigger battery, over the Surface Laptop 3, equalling to 16+ hours. This would mean no more dead laptop on the train, thus more opportunity to work!

Reliability

"MacOS is built by Apple and specially for Apple computers. Thanks to this, software and hardware work well together, making the MacBook very stable. Macs are also less susceptible to viruses." Need I say more?

Intuitivity

Apple's Mac OS feels very intuitive for me, which is why I find myself being able to work more accurately and more efficiently on a MacBook as opposed to Windows based machines.
